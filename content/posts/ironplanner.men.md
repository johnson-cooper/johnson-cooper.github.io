# ironplanner.men - Group Ironman Planner For Oldschool Runescape

**https://ironplanner.men** is a web-based planning and management tool designed specifically for Old School RuneScape (OSRS) Group Ironman teams. It enables groups to efficiently coordinate tasks, track progress, and achieve goals together.

## Key Features

- **Member Management**  
  Add and manage group members, assign roles, and track responsibilities. Each member can be color-coded for quick identification.

- **Task Management (Kanban Board)**  
  Create, edit, and organize tasks using a dynamic Kanban-style board. Tasks can be filtered by member or type, prioritized, and moved between columns (Backlog, In Progress, Done) via drag-and-drop.

- **Goals Tracking**  
  Set group goals with specific targets and deadlines. Goals include optional notes or strategies to guide members toward completion.

- **Wishlist / Item Tracking**  
  Maintain a shared list of items your group wants to acquire, track quantities, and mark items as acquired.

- **Notes & Strategies**  
  Store strategies, progress notes, and meeting notes in a centralized location. Notes are saved locally and included when sharing or exporting the planner.

- **Interactive Timeline**  
  Visualize tasks with due dates on an interactive timeline. Features zoom controls, today marker, and horizontal scrolling for easy navigation.

- **Progress Visualization**  
  View overall group progress with a doughnut chart showing tasks in Backlog, In Progress, and Done.

- **Share & Export**  
  Generate shareable links to share the current state of the planner or export/import the data as JSON for backup and collaboration.

## Technology

- Built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**  
- Uses **Bootstrap 5** for responsive layout and modals  
- **SortableJS** for drag-and-drop task management  
- **Chart.js** for progress visualization  
- Fully client-side with local storage, no server required  

---

**ironplanner.men** streamlines group coordination for OSRS Group Ironman players, helping teams stay organized, track tasks, achieve goals, and maximize efficiency in-game.
