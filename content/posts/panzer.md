# Panzer VST

**Year:** 2023  
**Created with:** Industry-level producers  

---

## Overview

**Panzer** is a **free sub bass sampler VST** designed for heavy, monster low-end sounds. It features **automated root notes** and **holds**, making it easy to create powerful sub bass lines.  

It also includes **distortion** and **amplification** parameters to enhance your sound and give it that monstrous edge.

**Platforms:** Windows / MacOS  

---

## Features

- Free sub bass sampler
- Automated root notes & holds
- Distortion and amplification controls
- Monster sound design ready
